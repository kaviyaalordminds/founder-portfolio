About Me

Visionary Entrepreneur & Digital Growth Strategist

Naveenkumar Samiyappan is an entrepreneur, digital marketing strategist, corporate trainer, and educator from Tamil Nadu, India. He is best known as the founder of Lordminds and Lordminds Academy, organizations focused on digital transformation, marketing, skill development, and career readiness. His public profile describes him as a Digital Marketing Strategist helping brands and institutions scale through AI, advertising, and conversion systems.

Lordminds was started in 2021 with the vision of helping businesses grow through digital marketing and technology. What began as a marketing-focused venture expanded into a broader ecosystem combining business growth, education, and technology — including digital marketing services, branding solutions, website development, software and CRM solutions, training and education, and AI and automation initiatives.
